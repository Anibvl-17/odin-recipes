Banana Muffins Recipe

Description

It's time for a sweet recipe! Let's prepare some delicious banana muffins.

Consider that this is an easy recipe for kids!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large ripe bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees CÂ°. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line cups with paper liners
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; set aside
  3. Mix bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a separate large bowl until well combined; fold in flour mixture until smooth
  4. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 2/3 full
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lighly pressed, about 25 to 30 minutes
  6. Cool briefly in the tin, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely
