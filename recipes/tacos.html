Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are a delicious meal that can have many ingredients. In this recipe, we're going to prepare Chicken Tacos.

You can prepare this recipe in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 Tortillas
  • Grated cheese
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 pound of corn
  • 1 spoon of oil
  • Your favorite sauces
  • Your favorite spices (salt, pepper, etc)

Steps

  1. Shred the chicken breasts and add the spices to it
  2. Heat up 1 spoon of oil in a pan
  3. Put the sliced chicken breasts in the pan and cook it
  4. Boil 500 ml of water and add the corn
  5. Dice the tomato
  6. Heat up the Tortillas for 1 minute
  7. Put 1 Tortilla in a plate and add the chicken, tomato, corn and grated cheese
  8. Close the Tortilla and repeat the steps with the other one
  9. Add the sauces on the top of the Tortillas
  10. And there it is, enjoy your meal!
