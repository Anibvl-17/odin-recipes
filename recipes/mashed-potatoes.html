Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

This mashed potatoes recipe will guide you to make perfect mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut in half lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup of mil
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water.
  2. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes, then return to the pot.
  4. Turn heat to high and allow potatoes to dry for about 30 seconds. Turn off heat
  5. Mash potatoes and add the milk and butter. Continue to mash until it is smooth.
  6. Whisk in salt and black pepper until evenly distributed, about 15 seconds
